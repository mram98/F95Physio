Treatment

Orthopedic Physiotherapy

Comprehensive musculoskeletal care addressing the full spectrum of bone, joint, and soft-tissue conditions. Our evidence-based protocols restore functional movement and eliminate pain at the root.

🔗
Neck Pain
Targeted cervical mobilisation and posture-correction therapy to relieve tension and restore full range of motion.
🦴
Back Pain
Spinal decompression and core stabilisation programs that address lumbar dysfunction and provide lasting relief.
💪
Shoulder Pain
Rotator cuff rehabilitation and scapular re-training to resolve pain and rebuild overhead strength and stability.
🦵
Knee Pain
Biomechanical correction, strengthening protocols, and manual therapy to restore pain-free knee movement and function.
🦴
Arthritis
Joint protection strategies and gentle progressive exercise to reduce inflammation, improve mobility, and manage chronic joint disease.
🔄
Frozen Shoulder
Phased stretching, joint mobilisation, and neuromuscular re-education to restore full capsular range in frozen shoulder cases.

✦ Return-to-Sport Focused
02
⚡ Focus: Safe Return to Sport

Sports Injury Rehabilitation

Specialised sports rehabilitation designed for athletes at all levels — from academy juniors to elite professionals. Our return-to-sport protocols are benchmarked against international standards.

🏃
Ligament Injuries
Structured ACL, PCL, and ankle ligament rehab with progressive loading to restore stability and athletic confidence safely.
💥
Muscle Tears
Graded therapeutic exercise and soft-tissue techniques to heal muscle tears and prevent recurrence under clinical supervision.
🦾
Tendon Injuries
Eccentric tendon-loading programs (Achilles, patellar, rotator cuff) proven to resolve chronic tendinopathy and restore elasticity.
🔁
Sports Overuse Injuries
Biomechanical correction and training-load management to treat overuse syndromes and prevent recurrence in active athletes.
Consult Our Specialists
✦ Neuro-Recovery Specialists
03
Rehabilitation

Neurological Rehabilitation

Intensive, goal-directed rehabilitation for individuals with neurological conditions, focused on maximising functional independence and quality of life through neuroplasticity-driven therapy.

🧠
Stroke Recovery
Task-specific movement retraining and constraint therapy to restore upper and lower limb function after stroke damage.
🔌
Spinal Cord Injury
Intensive mobility training, standing programmes, and assistive technology use to maximise independence after spinal cord injury.
⚖️
Balance Disorders
Vestibular rehabilitation and proprioceptive retraining exercises to correct balance deficits and reduce fall risk significantly.
🎯
Movement Coordination Problems
Precision motor retraining and coordination exercises to improve movement quality in conditions affecting the nervous system.
Consult Our Specialists
✦ Child-Centred Care
04
Paediatrics

Pediatric Physiotherapy

Child-friendly therapy programs delivered by specialists trained in paediatric movement science. We help children achieve developmental milestones and participate confidently in daily life.

📈
Developmental Delay
Structured motor milestone programs to help children catch up on delayed gross and fine motor skill development at the right pace.
🧍
Posture Issues
Early postural correction and ergonomic education to prevent scoliosis, kyphosis, and forward-head posture in growing children.
🎯
Coordination Problems
Fun, engagement-based proprioceptive and balance tasks to improve hand-eye coordination and sports readiness in children.
🚶
Movement Limitations
Guided therapeutic play and manual techniques to improve range of motion and mobility in children with movement restrictions.
Consult Our Specialists

Elderly Care

Geriatric Rehabilitation

Specialised physiotherapy programs designed with empathy and precision for elderly individuals — helping them regain strength, independence, and confidence in their daily lives.

⚖️
Balance Improvement
Progressive balance and stability training exercises tailored for elderly patients to restore confidence in everyday movement.
🛡️
Fall Prevention
Evidence-based multi-factorial fall prevention programs reducing fall risk through strength, balance, and reaction time training.
🔄
Joint Mobility
Gentle joint mobilisation and flexibility routines to ease stiffness, maintain range, and reduce arthritic pain in elderly joints.
💪
Strength & Functional Independence
Personalised resistance training and functional task practice to improve daily activity performance and independent living capacity.

✦ Therapeutic Warm Water Pools
06
💧 Aquatic Rehabilitation

Hydrotherapy

Rehabilitation exercises performed in therapeutic warm water pools harness the natural buoyancy of water to dramatically reduce joint stress while enabling movement and strength gains that would be impossible on land.

🦴
Arthritis
Warm water immersion reduces joint loading and pain, enabling arthritis patients to exercise with greater freedom and comfort.
🧠
Neurological Conditions
The supportive aquatic environment facilitates neuro-rehabilitation exercises that improve balance, coordination, and motor control.
🔩
Post-Surgical Rehabilitation
Early post-operative aquatic therapy accelerates recovery by enabling movement and strength work before full weight-bearing is safe.
👴
Elderly Patients
Safe, low-impact aquatic exercise ideal for elderly patients seeking improved mobility without risk of falls or joint overload.

Beyond General Physiotherapy

Specialty Clinics at F95

Dedicated clinical pathways for complex conditions that demand focused expertise. Each specialty clinic combines evidence-based protocols with advanced technology.

🧠
F95 Brain Gym

A movement-based cognitive health program that bridges the gap between physical training and brain performance — unique to F95 in Central India.

  • Brain-body coordination and neural pathway activation through structured movement
  • Memory and cognitive stimulation using progressive dual-task exercises
  • Balance and reaction training to improve neuromotor speed and agility
🤕
Headache & Migraine Clinic

A specialised physiotherapy approach targeting the cervical and postural causes of chronic headache — without relying on medication.

  • Chronic headaches & tension headaches
  • Migraines — cervicogenic trigger identification
  • Cervicogenic headaches from neck dysfunction
  • Cervical spine, posture, and muscle correction
😬
TMJ Dysfunction Clinic

Dedicated assessment and treatment of the temporomandibular joint — addressing jaw pain, clicking, and related headaches through precise manual therapy.

  • Jaw pain & facial discomfort management
  • Jaw clicking and locking resolution
  • Difficulty opening mouth rehabilitation
  • TMJ-related headache treatment
🩺
F95 Pelvic Health Clinic

A dedicated, private, and sensitive environment for men and women to address pelvic floor dysfunction — including pelvic chair therapy.

  • Urinary leakage and stress incontinence
  • Pelvic floor weakness assessment & rehab
  • Postpartum recovery programs
  • Pelvic pain — advanced pelvic chair therapy
🛡️
Injury Prevention Clinic

Proactive movement screening and correction programs designed to prevent injuries before they happen — for athletes, gym members, and runners.

  • Athletes & sports academy participants
  • Children in competitive sports programs
  • Gym members & fitness enthusiasts
  • Runners — gait and movement correction
