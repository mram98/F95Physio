Who We Are

Chhattisgarh's Most Impactful Physiotherapy Network

F95 Advance Physiotherapy & Research Centre is a unit of the HIRA CSR Foundation, dedicated to making world-class physiotherapy accessible across Central India. We deliver AI-driven analysis, evidence-based clinical care, and cutting-edge recovery modalities across clinics, corporate plants, and sports academies.

Our philosophy is simple: every body tells a story. We read it carefully — through advanced analysis and root cause assessment — before writing a single treatment plan. No guesswork. Just results.

"Eliminating pain at the root cause through advanced analysis and evidence-based care — because symptom management is never enough."

The Diagnostic Edge

We Test Before We Treat

Root Cause Analysis isn't a buzzword at F95 — it's a clinical protocol. Every patient undergoes comprehensive analysis before a treatment plan is written. Here's a glimpse:

🤖
Flagship Technology
AI-Based Movement Analysis
Computer-vision powered gait and biomechanical assessment that identifies dysfunctional patterns before they become chronic injuries. Real-time analysis. Clinically validated results.
👣
Foot Pressure Analysis
High-resolution plantar pressure mapping to detect postural imbalances and load distribution affecting the entire kinetic chain — from foot to spine.
🦴
Spinal Curvature Analysis
Digital spinal posture and mobility screening to identify the root cause of back pain, referred symptoms and postural deterioration.
⚡
Sports Readiness Screening
Evidence-based functional screening for return-to-play decisions, injury risk profiling and athletic performance baseline assessments.
Explore Our Full Diagnostic Protocol
Muscle function testing, sports screening, neurological assessment and more — all explained in detail.

Clinical Care

Specialized Treatment for Every Condition

Expert clinical physiotherapy spanning orthopedics, neurology, pediatrics and sports — delivered by 60+ qualified therapists across Chhattisgarh.

🦿
Orthopedic Physiotherapy
Musculoskeletal rehabilitation for joints, muscles, ligaments and post-surgical recovery. Arthritis, frozen shoulder, fracture rehab and more.
🧠
Neurological Rehabilitation
Stroke, Parkinson's, MS and spinal cord injury rehabilitation using neuroplasticity-based therapeutic protocols.
👶
Pediatric Physiotherapy
Gentle, play-based therapy for children with developmental delays, cerebral palsy, torticollis and musculoskeletal conditions.
🏃
Sports Rehabilitation
Elite sports injury management, performance enhancement and injury prevention for athletes from academies to professional sport.
