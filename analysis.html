Physical Assessment

Musculoskeletal Analysis

A comprehensive clinical evaluation to identify the root cause of pain, injury, or performance limitation through hands-on orthopedic testing.

Posture & Alignment

Systematic evaluation of spinal curvature, head position, and overall skeletal alignment in static standing posture.

Joint Mobility

Quantified range-of-motion testing across all major joints to identify restrictions contributing to movement dysfunction.

Muscle Imbalance

Comparative strength and length testing to detect asymmetrical muscle tension patterns that cause chronic pain cycles.

Functional Limitations

Activity-specific movement screening to map how structural findings translate into real-world performance barriers.

Soft Tissue Assessment

Specialized orthopaedic special tests to diagnose injuries to muscles, tendons, ligaments, and joint structures.

Joint Injury Tests

Evidence-based special tests for specific joint pathologies, including meniscal, ligamentous, and capsular integrity.

AI-Based Movement Analysis

03
Biomechanics

Foot Analysis

Precision assessment of foot structure, pressure distribution, and arch mechanics — the critical foundation of whole-body alignment.

Foot Pressure Analysis

Dynamic pedobarographic testing to study real-time pressure distribution under the feet during standing and walking. Identifies high-load zones, offloading patterns, and asymmetrical weight-bearing that cause pain and joint degeneration up the kinetic chain.

Foot Arch Analysis

Structural evaluation of foot arch height and alignment using multi-angle assessment. Accurately detects flat foot (pes planus), high arch (pes cavus), over-pronation, and supination — each of which impacts knee, hip, and lower-back health.

04
Performance Science

Sports Readiness & Injury Risk Screening

Technology-led performance assessment designed for children joining sports academies, active athletes, and gym-goers seeking to train safely.

Blaze Pod Analysis

Reaction-light system testing for agility, cognitive speed, and neuromuscular response latency under sport-specific conditions.

Witty Gate Analysis

Precision speed-gate timing system measuring sprint velocity, acceleration profiles, and linear speed benchmarks.

Opto Jump Analysis

Infrared jump analysis measuring flight time, contact time, and vertical power output for explosive strength profiling.

Human Track Analysis

Comprehensive locomotion tracking assessing movement quality, efficiency, and sport-specific movement patterns over distance.

Pelvic Health Program

Pelvic Health Assessment

Part of the F95 Pelvic Health Program — a specialised evaluation applicable to both men and women, addressing an often-overlooked dimension of musculoskeletal wellness.

Pelvic Floor Function

Clinical assessment of pelvic floor muscle strength, coordination, and endurance to diagnose dysfunction in both men and women.

Core Stability

Integrated evaluation of deep core musculature — transversus abdominis, multifidus, and diaphragm — and their role in pelvic stability.

Postpartum Health

Specialised musculoskeletal evaluation for new mothers addressing diastasis recti, pelvic girdle pain, and return-to-activity readiness.

Bladder Control Dysfunction

Assessment of urinary incontinence, urgency, and frequency patterns linked to pelvic floor weakness or hypertonicity.
