Precision-Driven Care

The F95 Way

Our honest, data-driven methodology helps patients regain mobility, eliminate chronic pain, and return to active life — safely and effectively.

Most people seek treatment only for pain relief. At F95, we look deeper. We invest time in understanding you — your history, your movement patterns, and your lifestyle — before a single therapy session begins. This is how we find the root cause, not just the symptom.

The 5% Promise

For the small fraction of cases that require medical, surgical, or alternative intervention, we provide responsible and transparent referrals — because your long-term wellbeing always comes first.

The Why

Mission & Vision

Two commitments that anchor every clinical decision we make.

Our Mission
🎯

Empowering You to Live Pain-Free

To treat pain from the root cause, empower our team with world-class training, and always put patients before profit. We are committed to building a healthcare community where every individual has access to honest, effective, and compassionate physiotherapy care.

Our Vision
🌍

Global Leadership in Musculoskeletal Care

To become a global leader in musculoskeletal care, driven by innovation, compassion, and excellence. We envision a world where every individual is empowered to live an active, pain-free life — regardless of background or circumstance.
