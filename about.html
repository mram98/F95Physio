Precision-Driven Care

The F95 Way

Our honest, data-driven methodology helps patients regain mobility, eliminate chronic pain, and return to active life — safely and effectively.

Most people seek treatment only for pain relief. At F95, we look deeper. We invest time understanding your history, movement patterns, and lifestyle before a single therapy session begins. This is how we find the root cause — not just the symptom.

This is the F95 Way: In-depth Root Cause Analysis, Detailed Movement & Postural Assessments, Evidence-Based Treatment Protocols, and Highly Specialized Manual & Exercise Therapy.

The 5% Promise

For the small fraction of cases that require medical, surgical, or alternative intervention, we provide responsible and transparent referrals — because your long-term wellbeing always comes first.
